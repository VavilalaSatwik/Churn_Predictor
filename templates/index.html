OTT Customer Subscription Churn Prediction



OTT (Over-The-Top) platforms are digital streaming services that deliver video content over the internet, bypassing traditional cable or satellite TV providers. Examples include Netflix, Hulu, and Disney+.

Customer churn, in the context of these platforms, refers to the prediction of whether subscribers are likely to cancel their subscriptions. By analyzing factors like viewing habits and customer support interactions, these platforms aim to foresee potential churn and implement strategies to retain customers. Effectively managing churn is crucial for sustaining business growth and enhancing user satisfaction in the competitive landscape of online streaming services.

How It Works: OTT Churn Prediction

Our OTT Churn Prediction system utilizes cutting-edge data science methods to examine past customer data. We trained models based on thorough evaluations of different demographic characteristics, and the best model was the XG Boost algorithm, producing dependable forecasts with a respectable degree of accuracy.

The Prediction Tool's Use:

Input client data into our user-friendly interface to determine if a customer is likely to leave. The main input fields and descriptions are listed below:

1. Weekly Minutes Watched:

Enter the customer's weekly average for the time spent watching material, facilitating the understanding of the user's platform interaction.( According to our data, ranges from 0 minutes to 530 minutes )

2. Customer Support Calls:

Enter the total number of calls made to customer care, that can be helpful in assessing the user's level of happiness and identifying possible problems. ( According to our data, ranges from 0 to 9 calls )

3. Multi-Screen:

Whether the user is watching your OTT platform in multiple screens or not.

By providing these details, our predictive model processes the information through the trained XG Boost algorithm to predict whether the customer will churn or not. Based on the results you can enable proactive measures to retain valuable customers if required.
